About Me

If someone would have come to me a few years ago and told me my life was about to take a complete turn, and that my days working at the Bank were numbered, I would have laughed it off to cover up the anxiety of the very thought. At that time, I had already spent nearly 10 years building a career in the financial industry and climbing the corporate ladder. So, the very thought that all of that would be gone, would most definitely not be something I saw as a positive impact on my future.

Even though the Bank provided me with years of experience, and a set of invaluable skills that have helped me get where I am today, there was something missing from my definition of success. Eventually, I came to realize the true meaning of Casey Neitstat’s quote. What I considered safe, was actually the most dangerous thing I could do. For that reason, I decided it was time for me to make a big change. It was time for me to discover my true passion and pursue my dream of working in the tech industry. A decision that has helped shape the person I am today.

So, who exactly am I? Well, I’m definitely more than just a self- taught Full-Stack Web Developer. I’m an individual who went through a massive personal and professional transformation over the past few years that I am extremely grateful for. I am also a self-motivated, passionate individual that doesn’t give up easily. I am a person that willingly gave up all comfort and financial security, knowing I needed, and wanted, to make a bigger contribution in this world. Also, I love spending time with my two spoiled dogs, exploring and traveling to new places, kickboxing, juicing and crystals. I push myself every day to learn something new, grow my faith and challenge myself to become a better person. This is just the start of my journey, and I’m excited to see what comes next.
